# CLAUDE.md — Marketing & SEO

## Project Overview
Combined marketing and SEO repository. Claude Code is configured with skills for content creation, conversion optimization, SEO, and growth strategy.

## What This Repo Covers
- SEO audits and content optimization
- Landing page copy and CRO
- Email campaigns and sequences
- Ad creative and paid media
- Social media content
- Launch and GTM strategy

## Conventions
- Copy drafts in `copy/`
- Email sequences in `emails/`
- Landing page content in `pages/`
- Campaign briefs in `campaigns/`
- SEO content in `seo/`

## Skills Available (~10, grouped by function)

### SEO (3)
- **seo-audit** — Technical SEO audits, rankings, Core Web Vitals
- **content-strategy** — Topic planning, keyword strategy, content calendar
- **schema-markup** — JSON-LD structured data, rich snippets

### Copy & Content (3)
- **copywriting** — Homepage, landing pages, pricing pages, feature pages
- **copy-editing** — Review and improve existing marketing copy
- **social-content** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram content

### CRO & Growth (2)
- **page-cro** — Conversion optimization for any marketing page
- **signup-flow-cro** — Registration and trial activation optimization

### Campaigns (2)
- **email-sequence** — Drip campaigns, lifecycle emails, nurture flows
- **launch-strategy** — Product launches, feature announcements, GTM

## Additional Skills (add per need, max 2-3)
- **paid-ads** / **ad-creative** — If running paid campaigns
- **ab-test-setup** — If A/B testing actively
- **marketing-psychology** — For persuasion/behavioral science angles
- **programmatic-seo** — For template pages at scale
- **cold-email** — For outbound sales
- **pricing-strategy** — For pricing/packaging decisions
- **referral-program** — For viral/referral loops
- **churn-prevention** — For retention focus
